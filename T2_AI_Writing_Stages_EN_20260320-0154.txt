[ACADEMIC PAPERS]

1. AI-Assisted Tools for Scientific Review Writing:
   Opportunities and Cautions
   Source : ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces, 2025
   URL    : https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/acsami.5c08837
   Summary: Evaluates AI tools specifically designed to assist with writing
            scientific review articles. Identifies concrete opportunities
            (literature synthesis, gap identification) while flagging
            cautions around hallucination, misattribution, and over-reliance
            that can compromise review quality.

2. Artificial Intelligence Tools for Research Writing
   Source : TESOL/EJ (EJ1478000), ERIC Full Text
   URL    : https://files.eric.ed.gov/fulltext/EJ1478000.pdf
   Summary: Surveys a broad set of AI tools applicable to different stages
            of research writing. Offers practical tips for educators and
            researchers, with stage-by-stage analysis of where AI adds
            value and where human judgment remains essential.

3. ChatGPT as an Academic Writing Tool: Factors Influencing
   Researchers' Intention to Write Manuscripts Using Generative AI
   Source : Manuel Garcia Lab / Peer-reviewed article
   URL    : https://manuelgarcia.info/publication/chatgpt-research-writing
   Summary: Survey-based study examining which factors (ease of use,
            perceived usefulness, trust, institutional norms) predict
            whether researchers intend to use ChatGPT for manuscript
            writing. Identifies stage-specific adoption patterns across
            drafting and revision tasks.
