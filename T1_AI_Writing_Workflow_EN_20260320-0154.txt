[ACADEMIC PAPERS]

1. The impact of generative AI on academic reading and writing:
   a synthesis of recent evidence (2023–2025)
   Source : Frontiers in Education, 2025
   URL    : https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/education/articles/10.3389/feduc.2025.1711718/full
   Summary: A synthesis of empirical studies published 2023–2025 examining
            how generative AI tools affect academic reading and writing
            practices. Reviews evidence on efficiency gains, quality
            trade-offs, and behavioral shifts among students and researchers.

2. This Paper Was Written with the Help of ChatGPT: Exploring the
   Consequences of AI-Driven Academic Writing on Scholarly Practices
   Source : EDM 2024 (Educational Data Mining), Short Papers
   URL    : https://educationaldatamining.org/edm2024/proceedings/2024.EDM-short-papers.55/index.html
   Summary: Reflexive study in which the authors themselves used ChatGPT
            throughout the writing process and analyze the consequences for
            scholarly conventions, voice, and attribution. Raises questions
            about what AI-assisted authorship means for academic norms.

3. ChatGPT in Academic Writing: A Scientometric Analysis of Literature
   Published Between 2022 and 2023
   Source : Sage Journals (Journal of Scholarly Publishing), 2025
            Gergely Ferenc Lendvai
   URL    : https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/15562646251350203
   Summary: Scientometric mapping of the rapidly growing body of literature
            on ChatGPT and academic writing. Identifies dominant themes,
            key journals, and citation networks in the field from its
            emergence through 2023.

   URL    : https://effortlessacademic.com/a-complete-guide-to-using-ai-for-academic-writing/

   Summary: Structured walkthrough for researchers on using AI to break a

            manuscript with AI assistance.

   Source : Thesify Blog
   URL    : https://www.thesify.ai/blog/write-scientific-paper-2025-ideas-first
   Summary: Argues for an "ideas-first" philosophy — using AI to clarify
